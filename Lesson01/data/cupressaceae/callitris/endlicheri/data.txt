Least Concern (IUCN 3.1)[1]  
  
Scientific classification  
Kingdom: Plantae  
Division: Pinophyta  
Class: Pinopsida  
Order: Pinales

Genus: Callitris  
Species:  C. endlicheri  
Binomial name

Callitris endlicheri, commonly known as the black cypress pine, is a species
of conifer in the Cupressaceae family. It is found only in Australia.
