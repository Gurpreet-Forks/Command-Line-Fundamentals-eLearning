Callitris rhomboidea, or Oyster Bay pine, is a species of conifer in the
family Cupressaceae.

It is found only in Australia. It is native to South Australia, Queensland,
New South Wales, Victoria and Tasmania,[2][3] and has also naturalised in
parts of Victoria and Western Australia.

One of the few islands it is found on is Taillefer Rocks in Tasmania.[4]
