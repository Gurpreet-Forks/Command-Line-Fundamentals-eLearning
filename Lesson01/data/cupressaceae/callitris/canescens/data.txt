Conservation status  
  
  
Least Concern (IUCN 3.1)[1]  
  
Scientific classification  
Kingdom: Plantae  
Division: Pinophyta  
Class: Pinopsida  
Order: Pinales  
Family: Cupressaceae  
Genus: Callitris  
Species:  C. canescens  
Binomial name  
Callitris canescens

Synonyms[2]  
  
  * Callitris morrisonii R.T.Baker
  * Frenela canescens Parl.

  
  
Callitris canescens is a species of conifer in the Cupressaceae family. It is
found only in Australia.
