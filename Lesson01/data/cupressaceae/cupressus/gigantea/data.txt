Cupressus gigantea, the Tibetan cypress, is a species of conifer in the
Cupressaceae family in Asia.

## Distribution[edit]

It is endemic and found only in Southeast Tibet \- China. Populations are
concentrated along the Yarlung Tsangpo River. Cupressus gigantea is the
biggest of all Cupressus species.

## King cypress[edit]

The biggest known specimen is the famous King Cypress, about 50 meters high,
5.8 meters in diameter, 0.165-acre (670 m2) of crown-projection-area; and
calculated age of 2,600 years.
