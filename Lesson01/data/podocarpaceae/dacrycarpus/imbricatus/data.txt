Dacrycarpus imbricatus is a species of conifer in the Podocarpaceae family. It
is found in Cambodia, China, Fiji, Indonesia, Laos, Malaysia, Papua New
Guinea, the Philippines, Thailand, Vanuatu, and Vietnam. It is a tall tree up
to 40 metres (130 ft) high.[2]
