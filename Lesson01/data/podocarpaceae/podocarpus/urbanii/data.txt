Conservation status  
  
  
Critically Endangered (IUCN 3.1)[1]  
  
Scientific classification

Division:  Pinophyta  
Class:  Pinopsida

Podocarpus urbanii is a species of conifer in the Podocarpaceae family. It is
found only in Jamaica.
