Podocarpus novae-caledoniae is a species of conifer in the Podocarpaceae
family. It is found only in Grande Terre and the Ile des Pins, New
Caledonia.[1]

## Habitat and Ecology[edit]

This species is usually found along watercourses running through maquis
shrublands.
