Podocarpus pendulifolius is a species of conifer in the family Podocarpaceae.
It is endemic to Venezuela. Its common names include Pino Carbon and Pino
Hayuco.[1]

This tree grows in rainforest habitat, sometimes at high elevation just below
the paramo. At elevation it may take a dwarfed form, but otherwise it may grow
to 20 meters in height.[1]
