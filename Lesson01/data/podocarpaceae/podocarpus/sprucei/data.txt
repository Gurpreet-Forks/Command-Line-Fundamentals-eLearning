Podocarpus sprucei is a species of conifer in the Podocarpaceae family. It is
found largely in Ecuador and Peru.

## Description[edit]

Tree to 20 m high (smaller at the highest elevations), with reddish brown
scaly bark.[1][2] Leaves elliptic to linear, dark green, stiff, 2-7 cm long,
apex acute, midrib on upper side a continuous shallow groove.[1] Male cones in
groups of 3-10 on 1.5-2.5 cm long peduncles, each cone up to 1 cm long.[1][2]
Seed cones axillary, solitary, purple-red when ripe; seed globose, 5-8 mm
long.[1]

## Distribution and habitat[edit]

P. sprucei can be found in cloud forests at 1800-3900 m of elevation in
Ecuador and northern Peru.[2][3]
