Podocarpus parlatorei is a species of tree in the Podocarpaceae family and
native to Argentina and Bolivia.[2][3]

## Description[edit]

Podocarpus parlatorei is a shrub or tree that grows up to 12 (occasionally 20)
meters high. Its leaves are linear to falcate, straight, 2.5-9 cm long, 2-3 mm
wide, with an acute pungent apex. Seeds globose, 5-6 mm long.[4]
