Podocarpus laubenfelsii is a species of conifer in the family Podocarpaceae.
It is native to Indonesia and Malaysia.[1]

This plant grows in four known locations and there are few mature trees in any
subpopulation. It grows alongside Agathis borneensis, Nageia wallichiana,
Sundacarpus amarus, Dacrydium gracile, and Falcatifolium falciforme in
forested habitat.[1]

This tree can grow quite large and is harvested for its valuable wood.[1]
