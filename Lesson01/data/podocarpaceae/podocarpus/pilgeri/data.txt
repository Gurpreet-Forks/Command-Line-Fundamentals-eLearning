Podocarpus pilgeri is a species of conifer in the Podocarpaceae family. It is
a small to medium-sized tree that is found in India and Bangladesh, Indochina
and Malay Archipelago. Its timber is hard and water-resistant.[2]

Podocarpus wangii of southern China is often treated as synonym of P.
pilgeri.[3]
