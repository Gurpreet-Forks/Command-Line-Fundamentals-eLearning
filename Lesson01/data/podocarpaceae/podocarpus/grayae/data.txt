Podocarpus grayae  
  
Conservation status  
  
  
Least Concern (IUCN 3.1)[1]  
  
Scientific classification  
Kingdom:  Plantae  
Division:  Pinophyta  
Class:  Pinopsida  
Order:  Pinales  
Family:  Podocarpaceae  
Genus:  Podocarpus

Binomial name  
Podocarpus grayae

Podocarpus grayae is a species of conifer in the Podocarpaceae family, endemic
to Australia. It is found only in the Northern Territory and Queensland
