Podocarpus globulus is a species of conifer in the family Podocarpaceae. It is
endemic to Malaysia, where there are only four known subpopulations, found in
Sabah and on the Sabahan border with Sarawak, although it may have
undiscovered populations elsewhere in these states or in neighbouring
Indonesia.[1]
