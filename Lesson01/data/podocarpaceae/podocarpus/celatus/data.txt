Podocarpus celatus is a species of conifer in the Podocarpaceae family. It is
found in the Amazon rainforest.

## Description[edit]

Trees 10-25 m high; with narrowly elliptic or oblong leaves, 5-15 cm long and
1-2 cm wide; male cones 2-3 cm long; seeds 5-6 mm long.[2][3]

## Distribution and habitat[edit]

P. celatus grows in lowland rainforests and low montane rainforests between
130 m and 1400 m of elevation in Brazil, Colombia, Peru, Ecuador, Venezuela
and Bolivia.[4][5]

## Ecology[edit]

This species grows on poor soil types: lateritic, sandstone and white sand.[4]
