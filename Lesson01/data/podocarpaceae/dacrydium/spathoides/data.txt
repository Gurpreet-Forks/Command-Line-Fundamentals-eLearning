Dacrydium spathoides  
  
Conservation status  
  
  
Near Threatened (IUCN 3.1)[1]  
  
Scientific classification  
Kingdom: Plantae  
Division: Pinophyta  
Class: Pinopsida  
Order: Pinales  
Family: Podocarpaceae  
Genus: Dacrydium  
Species:  D. spathoides  
Binomial name  
Dacrydium spathoides

Dacrydium spathoides is a species of conifer in the Podocarpaceae family. It
is found only in Indonesia.
