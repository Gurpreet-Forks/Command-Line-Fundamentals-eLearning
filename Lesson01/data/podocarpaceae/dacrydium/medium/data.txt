Dacrydium medium  
  
Conservation status  
  
  
Vulnerable (IUCN 3.1)[1]  
  
Scientific classification  
Kingdom: Plantae  
Division: Pinophyta  
Class: Pinopsida  
Order: Pinales  
Family: Podocarpaceae  
Genus: Dacrydium  
Species:  D. medium  
Binomial name

Dacrydium medium is a species of conifer in the Podocarpaceae family. It is
found in Indonesia and Malaysia.
