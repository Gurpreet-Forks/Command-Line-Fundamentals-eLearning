Dacrydium araucarioides is a species of conifer in the Podocarpaceae family.
It is found only in New Caledonia. It is a small tree that reaches a height
between 3 and 6 metres.[2]

The species is common and widespread, particularly in the south of Grand Terre
and along the east coast northwards to Houailou.[2]
