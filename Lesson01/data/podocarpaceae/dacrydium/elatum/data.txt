Dacrydium elatum is a species of conifer in the Podocarpaceae family. It is
found in Cambodia, Indonesia, Laos, Malaysia, Thailand, and Vietnam.[3]

It is a tree up to 30 metres (98 ft) tall, with dbh up to 80 centimetres (31
in).[4]
