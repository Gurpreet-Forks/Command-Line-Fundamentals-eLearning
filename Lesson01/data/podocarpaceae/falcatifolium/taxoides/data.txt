Falcatifolium taxoides is a species of conifer in the family Podocarpaceae. It
is found only in New Caledonia.

## Etymology[edit]

Falcatifolium means 'with sickle-shaped leaves', while taxoides means 'yew-
like' (resembling Taxus).[2]
