Falcatifolium angustum is a species of conifer in the Podocarpaceae family
found only in Malaysia. it can grow up to twenty meters tall.[2] It is
threatened by habitat loss,[3] in 1998 it was known to exist only in two
locations, both in Sarawak.[2]
