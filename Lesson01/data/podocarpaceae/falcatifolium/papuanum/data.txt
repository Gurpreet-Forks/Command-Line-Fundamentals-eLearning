Falcatifolium papuanum  
  
Conservation status  
  
  
Least Concern (IUCN 3.1)[1]  
  
Scientific classification  
Kingdom: Plantae  
Division: Pinophyta  
Class: Pinopsida

Falcatifolium papuanum is a species of conifer in the Podocarpaceae family. It
is found only in Papua New Guinea.
