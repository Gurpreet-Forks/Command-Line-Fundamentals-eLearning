Tsuga forrestii is a species of conifer in the Pinaceae family. It is
considered as a variety of Tsuga chinensis (i.e., T. c. var. forrestii) by
some.[2] It grows in mixed forests in mountains and valleys of northeast
Guizhou, southwest Sichuan, and northwest Yunnan, at altitudes of 2,000-3,000
m (6,600-9,800 ft).[2]

While not a target species, Tsuga forrestii is threatened by clear-cutting of
mixed forests.[1]
