Pinus krempfii, or Krempf's pine, is a rare species of pine, endemic to the
central highlands of Vietnam in the Da Lat-Nha Trang area.[2] It is unusual in
that it has flat needles. Because of this anomaly, it is put in its own
subsection, the Krempfianae.[3]
