Pinus pringlei, commonly known as Pringle's pine, is a species of conifer in
the Pinaceae family. It is found only in Mexico. The specific epithet,
pringlei, honours Cyrus Guernsey Pringle (1838-1911), an American botanist,
explorer and plant breeder.[2]
