Pinus jaliscana, the Jalisco pine, is a species of conifer in the Pinaceae
family.

It is endemic to southwestern Mexico. It is native to the Pacific slopes of
the Sierra Madre del Sur within western Jalisco state.

It is threatened by habitat loss.
