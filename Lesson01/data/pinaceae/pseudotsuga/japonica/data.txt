Pseudotsuga japonica, the Japanese Douglas-fir, is a species of conifer in the
pine family, Pinaceae, that is endemic to Japan. It is a medium-sized tree
growing to 25 m (82 ft) tall.[3] Japanese calls this tree 'Togasawara
(?????)'.[citation needed]
